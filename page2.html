From my heart…
>
I know I was not good to you in the past. I did not talk to you when you needed me, and no matter what the reasons were, it was my fault. I still feel guilty that I made you sad on your birthday. I did not give my best to make things right or to make you stay. But please know this: You have always mattered to me. You never deserved the hurt I caused. I was not mature enough back then to treasure someone like you. I missed you for those five years, and I still do. You are the reason I ever felt love in my life. You gave me so much happiness, and I failed to return the same. I am really lucky that you were part of my life. I am truly sorry for the pain I caused you even in your bday I made you feel hurt or alone. You deserved better from me.
